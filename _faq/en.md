## Frequently asked questions

Here are the answers to the questions we get asked most often. Can't find yours? Ask us via the contact form at the bottom of the page or by email at contact@la-villa-paris.com.

### Do the rooms have air conditioning?

Yes. All our rooms have air conditioning and heating, with individual temperature control in each room.

### Is wifi available?

Yes, a good wifi connection is available free of charge throughout the house and on the terrace.

### Do the rooms have a private bathroom?

Yes, every room has its own private bathroom with toilet, inside the room.

### What is breakfast like?

Every morning we serve a generous and varied breakfast: breads and pastries from local bakeries. Gluten-free, vegan and organic options are available on request.

### Is there parking nearby?

There is a paid car park a 5 minute walk from the house. Feel free to contact us before your arrival for more details.

### Is the house well located for visiting Paris?

Yes, the main sights are 15 to 30 minutes away by metro or bus. Access from the train stations and airports is quick and easy.

### How can we contact you?

By phone at +33 6 73 62 38 45, by email at contact@la-villa-paris.com or via the contact form at the bottom of the page. The house is located at 33 rue de la Fontaine à Mulard, 75013 Paris.
